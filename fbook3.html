Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

About the Book

Pride and Prejudice is a romantic novel by Jane Austen, first published in 1813. It is one of the most beloved works of English literature, celebrated for its sharp social commentary, memorable characters, and timeless exploration of love, marriage, and class. The novel offers a witty and insightful critique of Regency-era social norms while delivering a charming love story between two strong-willed protagonists.
