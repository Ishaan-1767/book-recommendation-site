Plot Summary

Anna Fox, a former child psychologist, lives alone in her New York City townhouse, trapped by her agoraphobia. She spends her days drinking wine, watching old films, and spying on her neighbors through her window. Her life takes a dramatic turn when the Russells, a seemingly perfect family, move in across the street. One night, Anna witnesses something horrifying: a violent crime in the Russells' house.

However, when Anna reports what she saw, her credibility is called into question. Haunted by her own past and struggling with mental illness, she becomes ensnared in a web of secrets and lies.
