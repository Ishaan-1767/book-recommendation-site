In the Woods

Author: Tana French

About the Book

In the Woods is the debut psychological mystery novel by Tana French, published in 2007. It is the first book in her Dublin Murder Squad series and combines elements of psychological suspense, crime fiction, and character-driven drama. The novel is known for its intricate plot, vivid characterization, and its exploration of memory, trauma, and identity.


Plot Summary

The story is narrated by Detective Rob Ryan, who is assigned to investigate the murder of a young girl, Katy Devlin, found in the woods near the small Irish town of Knocknaree. The case is particularly troubling because the murder site is eerily close to where, years earlier, Ryan and his two childhood friends disappeared. Ryan, who had returned to the town as a detective, was the only one of the three children to make it out alive, though he has no memory of the events that transpired.
