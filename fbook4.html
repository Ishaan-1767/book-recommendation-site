About the Book

The Great Gatsby is a classic novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, first published in 1925. Set during the Roaring Twenties, it explores themes of wealth, love, ambition, and the American Dream. Through its evocative prose and complex characters, the book paints a vivid picture of the Jazz Age while offering a poignant critique of the excesses and moral decay of the era. It has become a cornerstone of American literature and a perennial favorite among readers and critics alike.
