Big Little lies

Author: Liane Moriarty

About the Book

Big Little Lies is a captivating novel by Liane Moriarty, published in 2014. It seamlessly blends humor, drama, and suspense to explore themes of friendship, marriage, and the hidden struggles behind seemingly perfect lives. The book was adapted into a critically acclaimed HBO series in 2017, further cementing its popularity.


Plot Summary

The story follows Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who shoots her husband, Gabriel, five times in the face and then becomes completely mute. Her refusal to speak turns her into a mystery, capturing public fascination. Theo Faber, a criminal psychotherapist, becomes determined to unravel the truth behind her silence and the events leading up to the murder.
