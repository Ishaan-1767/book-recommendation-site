Before I Go to Sleep

Author: S.J. Watson

About the Book

Before I Go to Sleep is a psychological thriller by S.J. Watson, published in 2011. The novel centers around Christine Lucas, a woman with amnesia who wakes up every day with no memory of her past or her identity. Written in the first person, the novel takes readers into Christine's confused and fragmented world, blending psychological suspense with mystery. The book explores themes of trust, identity, and memory, keeping readers on the edge of their seats with every twist.


Plot Summary

Christine Lucas wakes up every morning not knowing where she is, who she's with, or even what she looks like.
