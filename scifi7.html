Snow Crash

Author: Neal Stephenson

About the Book

Snow Crash is a cyberpunk science fiction novel by Neal Stephenson, first published in 1992. The novel is a satirical yet thrilling exploration of a hyper-commercialized future dominated by virtual reality and corporate power. Known for its fast-paced narrative, sharp humor, and prescient depiction of digital culture, Snow Crash is a defining work of the cyberpunk genre and has inspired many modern tech concepts and cultural phenomena.


Plot Summary

The story is set in a dystopian future where the United States has fragmented into corporate city-states, and much of society exists in the Metaverse, a shared virtual reality space.
